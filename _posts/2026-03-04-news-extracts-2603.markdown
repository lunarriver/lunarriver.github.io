### China

#### https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/mar/03/china-two-sessions-president-xi-economy-defence-technology

The **standing** committee of China’s top political advisory body has voted to remove three generals from its ranks as a sweeping purge of the military continues before this week’s annual Two Sessions gathering.

```
standing ~ 常设的 -> 一直站在那的
```

**Collectively** the concurrent meetings are referred to as Two Sessions, one of the most important events in China’s political calendar when thousands of delegates arrive in Beijing.

```
collectively ~ 集体地、共同地 -> 把很多东西收集、聚集(collect)在一起
```

In 2018 it was the NPC that amended China’s constitution to **scrap** term limits for the president.

The **spectre** of military purges will hang over this week’s meetings. More than 100 senior officers had been purged or potentially purged since 2022, a **tally** that researchers have described as “**staggering**”.

```
spectre ~ 幽灵

tally ~ 记录，得分

staggering ~ 大得惊人的，令人吃惊的 -> stagger 兼有'蹒跚'和'使震惊'两种意思，其原意是：站不稳、摇摇晃晃、快要倒了；走路摇摇晃晃、东倒西歪，就是蹒跚；被吓傻了，脑子 “站不稳”，像喝醉一样晃，就是使震惊
```

Still, the opening of the Two Sessions on Wednesday and the NPC on Thursday will be full of **pomp** and circumstance.

```
pomp ~ 盛况 / pope 教皇
```

China wants to boost its ability to domestically produce the most advanced semiconductors, **blunting** the force of US sanctions designed to hold back China’s technological progress.

```
blunting ~ 减弱 -> 使变钝(blunt)
```

Outside of political **intrigue**, this year’s Two Sessions will reveal a number of economic indicators for the year ahead.

```
intrigue ~ 密谋、阴谋 -> intrigue 兼有'密谋'和'激起兴趣'的意思，其原意是：藏着秘密、让人猜不透；密谋、搞阴谋（坏的一面）；激起兴趣、勾起好奇心（好的一面）；
```

That may be suited to what Beijing considers an uncertain geopolitical future, particularly with regards to the US. But China’s domestic problems, such as high levels of youth unemployment and an ageing society, will not be cured by a doubling down on **niche**, specialist sectors, while major other parts of the economy, such as real estate, continue to **flail**.

```
niche ~ 原意是：墙上挖出来的小凹洞、壁龛；从'墙上凹进去的小空间'引申出：生态位(生物刚好适合生存的小环境)；小众市场/细分领域(只适合一小群人的位置)

flail ~ 原意是：连枷（古代农民用来打谷子、脱粒的工具）；引申出：乱挥、乱打(疯狂、无规则地挥来挥去)
```

reminiscent ~ 原意是：使人回想起的、勾起回忆的；（re - 再、回去 + minis / memor - 记得、想起 + escent - 有...性质的）
